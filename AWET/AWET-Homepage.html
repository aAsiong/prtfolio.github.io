AWET

an Alternative Web-based Education Tool designed to suppliment the teaching of Program Logic Formulation (PLF) to students with built-in environments for making pseudocode and flowchart.
Other than the built-in environments, AWET also offers lessons that coveers the fundamentals of PLF. This is only accessible for registered Students. Conversely, AWET also provides a platform and features for registered instructors.

If interested, proceed below

What AWET offers to Students

Join Classrooms

Registered Students can join classrooms and partake to activities created by the adviser of that classroom. Free PLF courses can be accessed through there.

Access Exercise Bank

Exercise bank contains problems that doesn't generate any score. Registered Students can freely answer theese problems for how many times they want until they accomplish the task.

Scorebook

Registered Students can track their progress by acceessing their scorebook in classrooms they have enrolled in. Progress are tracked via checking the modules, quizzes, and activities they have completed.
